===========================================
CARA DAFTAR GOOGLE reCAPTCHA
===========================================

LANGKAH-LANGKAH:
----------------

1. Buka: https://www.google.com/recaptcha/admin/create

2. Login dengan Google Account kamu

3. Isi form registrasi:
   - Label: Portfolio 4leafclover (atau nama apapun)
   - reCAPTCHA type: Pilih "reCAPTCHA v2" → "I'm not a robot" Checkbox
   - Domains: 
     * 4leafclover.id
     * www.4leafclover.id
     * localhost (untuk testing)
   - Accept reCAPTCHA Terms of Service: ✓ Centang
   - Send alerts to owners: ✓ Centang (optional)

4. Klik "Submit"

5. Kamu akan dapat 2 keys:
   - **Site Key** (untuk frontend) - contoh: 6LeIxAcTAAAAAJcZVRqyHh71UMIEGNQ_MXjiZKhI
   - **Secret Key** (untuk backend) - contoh: 6LeIxAcTAAAAAGG-vFI1TnRWxMZNFuojJ4WifJWe

6. SIMPAN kedua keys ini!
